About Me

Hi, my name is Prabhdeep Singh Khurana. I’m 16 years old and currently a student at Khalsa Secondary School in Surrey, BC, Canada. I moved to Canada from India in April 2018, and it’s been an exciting journey adjusting to a new life and culture.

I’m passionate about basketball — it’s more than just a game to me. I love spending my free time on the court, working on my skills and pushing myself to improve. My favorite player is the legendary Kobe Bryant — his dedication and mindset really inspire me to work harder in everything I do.

Academically, I’m really focused on my future. I’m passionate about mechanical engineering and hope to become a mechanical engineer one day. The idea of designing and working with complex machines fascinates me. Cars are a big part of that passion — my dream car is the powerful and sleek Hennessey Venom F5 Roadster. The speed and engineering behind it represent the kind of innovation I hope to be part of in the future.
